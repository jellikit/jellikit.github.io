MEET THE MURKOFF PRIME ASSETS (team)

Leland Coyle

Born in 1923 to a small Oklahoma town, Coyle is a cattle prod wielding cop, ready to shock (quite literally) any reagents that enter his trials.

Mother Gooseberry

Phyllis Futterman, or more known by her persona Mother Gooseberry, wields a duck-like drill named after her father and is ready to use it.

Franco Barbi

Franco was once the deformed son of a mafia don. After being kicked out by his father and brought to out facility, hes is now a gun carrying lunatic, ready to shoot down any reagents with his "Lupara".

Otto & Arora Kress

Otto and Arora are a pair of aristocratic conjoined twins, allowing them to spot Reagants from the front and back.
